Brief introduction of Luoyang Published by: Administrator Date: 2023-4-20

    Ancient capital Luoyang:
Luoyang, known as Yuzhou in ancient times, is located in the west of Henan Province and the middle reaches of the Yellow River. It is named for its location in the Yang of the Luo River. It is one of the first historical and cultural cities announced by The State Council and an important industrial city in the central region.
Luoyang has a civilization history of more than 5,000 years, a city history of 4,000 years and a capital history of more than 1,500 years. It is one of the birthplaces of Chinese civilization and one of the birthplaces of the Chinese nation. It was an important hub of the Grand Canal of Sui and Tang Dynasties. Peony is famous in the world for Luoyang, known as the "millennium imperial capital, Peony flower city". Luoyang has been honored as China's Outstanding Tourist City, National Civilized City, Top Ten Most Charming Cities in China and so on. In 2014, Luoyang city was selected as the national comprehensive pilot of new urbanization.
As of 2015, Luoyang has three World cultural heritage sites, along the two sides of the Luohe River distribution of the Xia capital Erlitou site, Yanshi Mall, East Zhou City, Han and Wei City, Luoyang City Sui and Tang Dynasty five major capital sites.
